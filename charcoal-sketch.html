Charcoal Sketching

Overview: Charcoal sketching is known for its bold and dramatic appearance. It uses charcoal sticks, pencils, or powdered charcoal to create intense contrasts and textures.
Tools:
  • Charcoal pencils or sticks
  • Powdered charcoal
  • Kneaded erasers
  • Fixative spray
Techniques:
  • Smudging: Use fingers or blending tools to create smooth gradients.
  • Highlighting: Remove charcoal with an eraser to add highlights.
  • Contrast Building: Focus on the interplay of dark and light areas.
Applications:
  • Portraits
  • Landscape art
  • Abstract art
Tips:
  • Use fixative spray to prevent smudging.
  • Experiment with different types of charcoal to find your preferred style.
